What is a hackathon?

You've probably heard this term being thrown about but what is it? A hackathon is basically an event for you and your fellow developers to get together, come up with the "next big thing," build it, and pitch it! TrojanHacks is especially for people who have never done this before and is a springboard for larger university hackathons like CalHacks and HackSC. Learn more about hackathons here: https://mlh.io/

Collaborating Organizations

Scope USC

Scope is a computer science student organization dedicated to fostering a more cohesive structure for student-developers to build independent team-projects and contribute to the open source community.

AAAI

AAAI (Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence) aims to support students who have a desire to learn about and apply concepts from Artificial Intelligence. We organize industry and academic tech-talks, host movie nights, and develop AI coding challenges that expose students to new technologies and that challenge them to try something new.

USC ACM Siggraph

Siggraph is dedicated to the generation and dissemination of information on computer graphics and interactive techniques.

Makers at USC

Got a knack for electronics? We'll help you with getting your ideas off the ground. From wearables to logic circuits, we provide members with the knowledge, materials and connections to make something awesome.
